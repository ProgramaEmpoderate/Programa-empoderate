Mission

We are a social program focused on empowering academic excellence in teenagers with an effort with different basic subjects, at the same time, developing their abilities and skills through the workshop that helps the development inside of society.




Vision

To be a formative social of leadership that can provide ideas and actions to solve problems inside their community and country.




Objective - social impact

Provide academic opportunities and personal development, knowledge in emotional intelligence, and practical workshops. Furthermore, return a little help that as volunteers we received, help adolescents can opt for better opportunities in their high school or seek forms of self-employment in case they need it.




Our values

Gender equity

Consciousness in the teenagers that all can have a full exercise of human rights of sex, age, sexual orientation, belief, or any other condition and promoting in them the eradication of all forms of discrimination within society.


Respect

As teenagers we value human diversity and likewise encourage to value that dignity and individuality of each person.


Solidarity

We empathize as young people with the needs and problems there are in society.


Teamwork

We encourage teamwork inside and outside the classroom so that they can share ideas and tools to solve any doubt or conflict.


Multiplicative effect

Replicate what they have learned in their community, and create a value chain, becoming agents of change and creating an impact on the country's development.
