About us

Empodérate is an initiative of "Believe" through of challenge YLAI FORTALECE. having an openness on June 8th, 2019. The project consists in different training workshops that support teenagers, especially women of secondary school in República de Haití School in San Antonio del monte city in the areas of English, IT, personal development (emotional education and vocational), professional development (arts and crafts, recycling, leadership, SDG, etc.) Due to the city economic development being few and the population trying to look for new opportunities at an early age. We want to provide these tools to adolescents and give them the opportunities that they need. For that reason, we select between 30 to 60 students to attend the classes on Saturdays from 1:30 pm to 5:30 pm for a period of 10 months.

This project began based on the support of our team and donation from the town hall (project promotion and areas for work), school (classrooms, computer classrooms, and computers), social media management (We did an announcement and many people attend to give material donations for the project). Moreover, we are going to continue to manage through donations to different entities, also it was agreed with the town hall to participate in a freeway in fairs to recollect funds and we also have the support of several businesses that are going to lend to sell products and provide a percentage for the project each year. This 2020 we are going to have the support of ¡Supérate! Program and the delivery of the Solidarity award.

Our services

Our program provides:

01

We offer opportunities of high-quality education to low-income youth through our various workshops (math, English, It, etc.)

02

We form agents of community change who offer solutions and involve in the development of the community, this through SDG workshops and emotional intelligence.

03

We work a lot in the topic of female empowerment, gender equity and we instruct the girls they can be who they want if they fight for their dreams.

04

We support youth to applying for scholarships, we advise and help them to complete documents so they can apply, so a lot of them can follow their studies.
